The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our Luxury Yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electiricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge

How are yurt furnished?

What should I bring?

Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing
